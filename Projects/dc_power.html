DC Power Supply

A dc power supply that delivers 10 mA at 3V ± 0.1V from a source that is 120V (rms) at 1 kHz

Overview

Like other projects that I’ve worked on, this too was for a class. We were given a very specific requirement of making a dc power supply that delivers 10 mA at 3V ± 0.1V from a source that is 120V (rms) at 1 kHz using only hardware. We had to design, build, and test the circuit via the AD3 to ensure that iWt was performing as per the requirements.

Tools & Technologies

  • AD3
  • Wavelenghts
  • Circuit Design
  • Circuit Simulation

Challenges

In comparison to other things that I have made, I found this to be on the simpler side as I didn’t face many significant issues. I was able to design the circuit fairly quickly as it was simple consisting of just four components total.
